“The Moment”

Each day we live our lives waiting for “the moment”
The moment when your life “really starts”
The moment when you’ll really be happy
The moment where all your problems fade away


But
There is only one real truth
And that is
That “the moment” is a lie
Problems do not fix themselves
Lives don’t wait to start
Time stops for no man


Each day we live our lives in an ever moving world
Every moment flows into the next
Every empty feeling
Every low moment
Fades
Every wide smile
Every happy day
Fades


The best you can do is keep moving with the river’s flow
Instead of drowning in it


Your life has already started
Cherish it
Don’t worry


I believe in you
